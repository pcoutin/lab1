Bamboo bow

Emulating the Bhutanese bamboo bows, made by tying two bamboo slats, then tapering and tillering. The biggest differences are that the outside of the bamboo is the back (away from the archer) rather than the belly of the bow, and it's wider than the Bhutanese bows. Bends too much at the handle... I also had to make it shorter because it was too weak. The bamboo is too thin.
